Expedia

Expedia is a well-known travel booking platform that allows users to search for flights, hotels, and car rentals. It supports flights to and from multiple countries around the world and allows users to customize their search parameters, such as the departure location and travel dates.

Link: https://www.expedia.com.ph

Skyscanner

Skyscanner is a travel search engine that compares prices for flights, hotels, and car rentals from multiple providers. It supports flights to and from multiple countries and allows users to search for flights based on flexible dates or nearby airports.

Link: https://www.skyscanner.com.ph

Kayak

Kayak is a travel search engine that allows users to search for flights, hotels, and car rentals. It supports flights to and from multiple countries and allows users to filter their search results based on various criteria, such as the airline or departure time.

Link: https://www.kayak.com

Google Flights

Google Flights is a flight search engine developed by Google that allows users to search for flights to and from multiple countries. It allows users to filter their search results based on various criteria, such as the airline, departure time, or price range.

Link: https://www.google.com/travel/flights
